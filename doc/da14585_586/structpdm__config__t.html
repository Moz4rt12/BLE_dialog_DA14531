◆ src_direction

pdm_src_direction_t pdm_config_t::src_direction

The direction of the sample rate converter. For example, when direction is PDM_DIRECTION_IN and src_direction is PDM_SRC_DIRECTION_PCM the PDM signal will be passed to the PCM interface. Or when the PDM direction is out and src_direction is PDM_SRC_DIRECTION_REG the SRC I/O registers are used for PDM input.

◆ src_sample_rate

unsigned int pdm_config_t::src_sample_rate

The sample rate of the sample rate converter (Hz). This is only used when src_direction is PDM_SRC_DIRECTION_REG.
