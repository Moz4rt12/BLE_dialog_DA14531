Note
The PD_TIM domain shall be functional prior to using Timer1: SetBits16(PMU_CTRL_REG, TIM_SLEEP, 0); while ((GetWord16(SYS_STAT_REG) & TIM_IS_UP) != TIM_IS_UP);
