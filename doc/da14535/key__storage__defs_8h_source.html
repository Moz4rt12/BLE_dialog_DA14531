109 /*
110 * The storage areas are above the OTP CS area for DA1453x ICs and above the OTP header area for DA1458x ICs
111 * +------------------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
112 * | ############################ | DA14531 / DA14531-01 | DA14585 / DA14586 | DA14535 |
113 * +------------------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
114 * | AES Keys and IV storage area | 7F87CC8 | 7F8FBD0 | 7F82CC8 |
115 * | ECC keys storage area | 7F87D68 | 7F8FC70 | 7F82D68 |
116 * | AES Revokation area | 7F87EA8 | 7F8FDB0 | 7F82EA8 |
117 * | ECC Revokation area | 7F87EBC | 7F8FDD8 | 7F82EBC |
118 * +------------------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
119 *
120 *
121 * The AES Keys and IV storage area consists of 5 Key and IV pair entries which are assumed to be placed in the following addresses:
122 * +------------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
123 * | ###################### | DA14531 / DA14531-01 | DA14585 / DA14586 | DA14535 |
124 * +------------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
125 * | AES Key and IV Entry 0 | 7F87CC8 | 7F8FBD0 | 7F82CC8 |
126 * | AES Key and IV Entry 1 | 7F87CE8 | 7F8FBF0 | 7F82CE8 |
127 * | AES Key and IV Entry 2 | 7F87D08 | 7F8FC10 | 7F82D08 |
128 * | AES Key and IV Entry 3 | 7F87D28 | 7F8FC30 | 7F82D28 |
129 * | AES Key and IV Entry 4 | 7F87D48 | 7F8FC50 | 7F82D48 |
130 * +------------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
131 *
132 * Each entry consists of a 128-bit AES key and an 128-bit AES IV:
133 * +----------------------+-------------------+--------------------+
134 * | AES Key and IV Entry | AES Key (16 bytes) | AES IV (16 bytes) |
135 * +----------------------+-------------------+--------------------+
136 *
137 *
138 * The ECC Keys storage area consists of 5 64-byte ECC keys:
139 * +-----------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
140 * | ######### | DA14531 / DA14531-01 | DA14585 / DA14586 | DA14535 |
141 * +-----------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
142 * | ECC key 0 | 7F87D68 | 7F8FC70 | 7F82D68 |
143 * | ECC key 1 | 7F87DA8 | 7F8FCB0 | 7F82DA8 |
144 * | ECC key 2 | 7F87DE8 | 7F8FCF0 | 7F82DE8 |
145 * | ECC key 3 | 7F87E28 | 7F8FD30 | 7F82E28 |
146 * | ECC key 4 | 7F87E68 | 7F8FD70 | 7F82E68 |
147 * +-----------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
148 *
149 * Each entry consists of 256-bit Ux,Uy ECC keys (64 bytes in total):
150 * +---------------+---------------------------+
151 * | ECC key entry | ECC Public Key (64 bytes) |
152 * +---------------+---------------------------+
153 *
154 *
155 * The AES Revokation area consists of 5 revokation flags that will be written from the bootloader
156 * if a downloaded signed and verified image requests to revoke a certain AES key set (IV and Key)
157 * A revoked AES key will not be used for decryption in future decryption procedures
158 * +-----------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
159 * | ##################### | DA14531 / DA14531-01 | DA14585 / DA14586 | DA14535 |
160 * +-----------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
161 * | AES Revokation Flag 0 | 7F87EA8 | 7F8FDB0 | 7F82EA8 |
162 * | AES Revokation Flag 1 | 7F87EAC | 7F8FDB8 | 7F82EAC |
163 * | AES Revokation Flag 2 | 7F87EB0 | 7F8FDC0 | 7F82EB0 |
164 * | AES Revokation Flag 3 | 7F87EB4 | 7F8FDC8 | 7F82EB4 |
165 * | AES Revokation Flag 4 | 7F87EB8 | 7F8FDD0 | 7F82EB8 |
166 * +-----------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
167 *
168 *
169 * The ECC Revokation area consists of 5 revokation flags that will be written from the bootloader
170 * if a downloaded signed and verified image requests to revoke a certain ECC key.
171 * A revoked ECC key will not be used for verification in future verification procedures
172 * +-----------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
173 * | ##################### | DA14531 / DA14531-01 | DA14585 / DA14586 | DA14535 |
174 * +-----------------------+----------------------+-------------------+---------+
175 * | ECC Revokation Flag 0 | 7F87EBC | 7F8FDD8 | 7F82EBC |
176 * | ECC Revokation Flag 1 | 7F87EC0 | 7F8FDE0 | 7F82EC0 |
